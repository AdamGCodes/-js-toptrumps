Welcome to Card Trumps

How to Play

  • Starting the Game: Click "Start Game" to shuffle and deal the cards. A random player goes first.
  • Taking Turns: Choose a category on your card; the CPU selects randomly. Higher value wins. After each hand, click "Next Hand" button to play the next hand.
  • Winning and Draws: Winner takes both cards. Draws go into a pot, claimed by the next round’s winner.
  • Game End: The game continues until one player has all the cards and wins. Alternatively, clicking "End Game" manually determines the winner based on who has the most cards.

Click the “X” to close this guide and start playing!
