Top Trumps

How To Play

Top Trumps is a card game where each player has a hand of cards featuring different subjects (like cars, animals, or superheroes). Each card has statistics in categories, such as speed, strength, or intelligence. Players take turns choosing a category and comparing the values on their cards. The player with the highest value in the chosen category wins the round and takes the other players' cards. The game continues until one player collects all the cards or has the most cards when the game ends.

  1. To start the game click the start game button.
  2. The game will start on Player 1's turn (That's you!)
  3. You'll need to check out your card and decide which category you are going to select for this hand
  4. Once you have made your selection check the message box to see the result of that hand
  5. If you win, you get both of the cards from that hand and take another turn until you lose a hand
  6. If you lose, play will pass to the computer. You won't be able to make a selection.
  7. When play passes to the computer a (Next Hand) button will appear. To have the computer make it's selection press it.
  8. If you win a hand on the computer's turn play will pass back to you, you'll be able to make selections and the (Next Hand) button will dissapear.
  9. Play continues like this until eithe someone wins all the cards and is declared the winner or
  10. You hit the End Game button and the game is ended then and there. If ending the game manually the decks will be counted up and the player with the most cards at that time declaired the winner

Enjoy your game!
